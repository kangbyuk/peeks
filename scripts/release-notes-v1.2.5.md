## PEEKS v1.2.5

### Highlights
- **FIFA World Cup (`fifa.world`)**: dedicated **🏆 WC** flows separate from club soccer — home cards, ALL GAMES, standings (group + knockout bracket), national team picker in settings.
- **Next game**: when ESPN team `nextEvent` is empty (common for WC), fallback uses scoreboard window so national teams show upcoming matches (e.g. June 2026).
- **UI**: WC standings sub-tabs match PEEKS styling; settings WC hint removed; tighter spacing; tab labels (Soccer, WC, baseball) aligned.

### Assets
- `PEEKS-1.2.5-universal.dmg` (macOS universal)
- `PEEKS-Setup-1.2.5.exe` (Windows installer)
